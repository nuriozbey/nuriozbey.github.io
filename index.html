My Story

I’m an enthusiastic data scientist with a theoretical background in machine learning algorithms. My career journey started with developing machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms for image processing applications in biomedical and UAV systems.

After completing my undergraduate education, I started working in a white goods factory that produces big data every second. Doing research and development work with big data collected in this environment turned me into a data scientist.

I love helping others develop open source applications. Therefore, designing algorithms that make others' jobs easier and contributing to other studies around the world allowed me to discover my passion for data science and artificial intelligence.

My master's degree education allowed me to deepen my technical knowledge and gain expertise. I constantly continue my lifelong learning journey and support my continuous development with ongoing leadership and personal development trainings
